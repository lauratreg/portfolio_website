2017

Linea Body Works

Massage Therapy - San Francisco, CA

It has been exactly 1 month since I first set eyes on HTML. I had the pleasure of designing and developing a website for Kendra of Linea Body in San Francisco, California. This project was a total brand refresh from logo to finished site. We took her exisiting Wordpress site down and put a temporary landing page up while the site was being built. She wanted to up her website game by creating a clean and modern website with touches of blue and gold. Challenge accepted! A one page scrolling website will give her clients one place to connect,learn more, and book appointments.
